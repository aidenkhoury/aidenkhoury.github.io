A quick reference about CSS Colors.

CSS Colours are specified in the stylesheet and are able to interact with the page in 2 seperate ways. The foreground and the background.

The foreground is the main part of the element, such as the colour of the text or the colour of a line, whereas the background colour is everything EXCEPT the foreground.

e.g. Red in the color property (foreground) but white in the background-color property.
